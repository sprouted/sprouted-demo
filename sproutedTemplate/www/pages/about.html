PeakEd. Stay on top

=======

This app was developed by the Coding Club (collaborators including: Tahmidul Alam, Sumit Das, William Doyle, Danielle Simon, Ellie Tien, and Mehdad Zaman) and with the guidance, help, and support of our wonderful advisor Ms. Tanzosh!

The goal of this app is to consolidate all important Nest+m events into one central location. If you happen to miss morning anouncments, you'll be able to easily view any upcoming Nest+m events on our app!

Keep track of club events, sports games, schoolwide events, and school holidays by viewing the main page.

We would love to get any feedback on the app so please email dragondcs28@gmail.com or ellielicious00@gmail.com. We hope you enjoy the app!
